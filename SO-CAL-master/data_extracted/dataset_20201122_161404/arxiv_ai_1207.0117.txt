The use of Artificial Intelligence is finding prominence not only in core computer areas, but also in cross disciplinary areas including medical diagnosis.
In this paper, we present a rule based Expert System used in diagnosis of Cerebral Palsy.
The expert system takes user input and depending on the symptoms of the patient, diagnoses if the patient is suffering from Cerebral Palsy.
The Expert System also classifies the Cerebral Palsy as mild, moderate or severe based on the presented symptoms.
