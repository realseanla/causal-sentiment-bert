Automatic detection of sarcasm has witnessed interest from the sentiment analysis research community.
With diverse approaches, datasets and analyses that have been reported, there is an essential need to have a collective understanding of the research in this area.
In this survey of automatic sarcasm detection, we describe datasets, approaches (both supervised and rule-based), and trends in sarcasm detection research.
We also present a research matrix that summarizes past work, and list pointers to future work.
