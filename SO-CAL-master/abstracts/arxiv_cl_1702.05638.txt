This paper reports on a writing style analysis of hyperpartisan (i.e., extremely one-sided) news in connection to fake news.
It presents a large corpus of 1,627 articles that were manually fact-checked by professional journalists from BuzzFeed.
The articles originated from 9 well-known political publishers, 3 each from the mainstream, the hyperpartisan left-wing, and the hyperpartisan right-wing.
In sum, the corpus contains 299 fake news, 97 percent of which originated from hyperpartisan publishers.
