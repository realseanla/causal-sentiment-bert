This article tackles a number of technical issues related to the AI definition.
These include the format of the data, the Undef and Nothing symbols, different ways of defining the meaning of life, as well as the introduction of the concept of "incorrect move".
These are issues of no material importance, if we are interested in the AI definition from a purely theoretical point of view; however, if we want to develop a real program satisfying that definition, we are to find these issues important.
