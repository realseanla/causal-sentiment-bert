The past few years have seen a tremendous growth in the popularity of smartphones.
As newer features continue to be added to smartphones to increase their utility, their significance will only increase in future.
Combining machine learning with mobile computing can enable smartphones to become 'intelligent' devices, a feature which is hitherto unseen in them.
Also, the combination of machine learning and context aware computing can enable smartphones to gauge user's requirements proactively, depending upon their environment and context.
Accordingly, necessary services can be provided to users.
