The development of expert system for treatment of Diabetes disease by using natural methods is new information technology derived from Artificial Intelligent research using ESTA (Expert System Text Animation) System.
The proposed expert system contains knowledge about various methods of natural treatment methods (Massage, Herbal/Proper Nutrition, Acupuncture, Gems) for Diabetes diseases of Human Beings.
The system is developed in the ESTA (Expert System shell for Text Animation) which is Visual Prolog 7.3 Application.
The knowledge for the said system will be acquired from domain experts, texts and other related sources.
